How to disconnect Arcline from QuickBooks Online

You can revoke Arcline’s access to your QuickBooks Online data at any time. Disconnecting stops new data from being shared with Arcline while preserving your ability to request deletion of historical data in line with our Privacy Policy.

Step-by-step disconnect instructions

To disconnect Arcline from your QuickBooks Online company:

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online. Use the same account that originally connected Arcline.
  2. Open Apps > My Apps. Locate Arcline in your list of connected applications.
  3. Select the Arcline app. Choose the option such as Disconnect, Disconnect app, or similar.
  4. Confirm the disconnect. Follow the prompts from Intuit/QuickBooks to confirm that you want to revoke Arcline’s access.

If the QuickBooks interface changes or you cannot locate the disconnect option, email us at ben14parks@gmail.com and we will provide updated instructions.

What happens after you disconnect

  • No new data pulls. After disconnection, Arcline will no longer request, receive, or refresh data from the disconnected QuickBooks company.
  • Read-only access only. Arcline never writes to, modifies, or deletes records in your QuickBooks account. Disconnecting only affects our ability to read future data.
  • Existing analyses may remain. Readiness scores, metrics, and reports that were generated before disconnection may continue to be stored in Arcline’s systems for the purposes described in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
  • Reconnecting is optional. You can choose to reconnect at any time to resume updated AI readiness analyses, or you may remain disconnected permanently.

Data retention & your rights

We retain customer data only for as long as it is necessary to provide analysis and advisory services. If an engagement ends or a customer requests deletion, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

We do not store or retain financial system credentials, and we never sell or share customer data with third parties for their own marketing or commercial use. For details on our practices, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Where required by law and subject to applicable exceptions, you may have the right to request:

  • Access to the information we hold about you.
  • Correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion of certain information.

To exercise these rights or request deletion of historical data held by Arcline, please contact us at ben14parks@gmail.com. We will respond in accordance with our policies and applicable law.

Questions or need help?

If you are unsure whether Arcline is currently connected to your QuickBooks company, need help completing the disconnect steps, or would like to understand how we handle your data, reach out and we’ll assist you directly.
