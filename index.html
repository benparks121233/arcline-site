Workflow

How Arcline works with your systems

The current pilot focuses on QuickBooks Online for SMB construction companies. We compute data-quality and readiness metrics, then generate an executive summary, ROI-anchored roadmap, and CEO-friendly narrative – all stored back in your Airtable base.

1. Connect & authorize

You authorize Arcline to securely read accounting data from QuickBooks Online via Intuit’s OAuth flow. Tokens are stored in Airtable and refreshed securely.

  • Read-only access to relevant accounting data
  • Revocable by you at any time

2. Compute metrics & readiness scores

The backend computes metric bands and pillar scores for ledger reliability, operational freshness & hygiene, and automation enablement.

  • Required field completeness
  • Days since last transaction & error rates
  • Reconciliation, job costing & digital invoicing usage

3. Generate narrative & ROI roadmap

Using your metrics and owner-provided ROI inputs, Arcline builds a CEO-level narrative, ranked “priority fixes,” and a simple roadmap with modeled ranges.

  • No invented savings numbers
  • Outputs grounded in your data and inputs
Signals

What we analyze in QuickBooks Online

Arcline does not replace your accountant or bookkeeper. Instead, it measures how ready your existing data is for reporting, analytics, and automation.

Ledger reliability

  • Consistency of reconciliations across key accounts
  • Error incidence per 1,000 transactions
  • Use of manual journals vs. system-driven entries

Operational freshness & hygiene

  • Days since last posting activity
  • Required field completeness on transactions
  • Attachment coverage for invoices, bills, and expenses

Automation enablement

  • Share of invoices issued digitally vs. manually
  • Job / project costing usage for profitability insight
  • Signals that future AI workflows can safely depend on
